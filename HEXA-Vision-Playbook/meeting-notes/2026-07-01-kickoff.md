## Discussion

### 1. Project Vision
The goal is to create an immersive 3D architectural visualization platform. The key differentiator is the "Awwwards-level" visual fidelity combined with a full business ERP (Odoo).

### 2. Tech Stack
- Next.js 15 for frontend.
- NestJS for BFF.
- Strapi 5 for CMS.
- Odoo 17 for business ops.
- Three.js/R3F for 3D.
- Decisions: Monorepo approved. Docker Compose for initial deploy.

### 3. Repository Strategy
 Agreed on a monorepo with npm workspaces. All shared types in `/packages/types`.

### 4. Sprint 0
Priority is the Playbook. No coding until the operating manual is finished.
