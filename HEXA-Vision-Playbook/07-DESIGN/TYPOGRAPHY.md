# ✍️ TYPOGRAPHY: THE VOICE OF PRECISION

**Version:** 1.0 | **Scope:** Design $\rightarrow$ Frontend | **Standard:** Architectural / Clean

## 1. THE TYPOGRAPHIC PHILOSOPHY
Typography is the "voice" of the lauch. We use a combination of **Bold Architectural Headings** and **Clean, Technical Body Text** to convey a sense of authority and precision.

---

## 2. THE FONT STACK

### I. Primary Heading: "The Statement"
- **Font:** `Inter` (Bold / Extra Bold) or a custom high-end Sans-Serif.
- **Characteristics:** High X-height, geometric, authoritative.
- **Usage:** H1, H2, and key hero statements.
- **Styling:** Tight letter-spacing (`-0.02em`) for a more "compact" and modern look.

### II. Body Text: "The Detail"
- **Font:** `Inter` (Regular / Medium).
- **Characteristics:** Extremely legible, neutral, balanced.
- **Usage:** Paragraphs, captions, and UI labels.
- **Styling:** Standard letter-spacing, generous line-height (`1.6`) for readability.

### III. Technical Accent: "The Blueprint"
- **Font:** `JetBrains Mono` or a similar high-end Monospace font.
- **Characteristics:** Precise, technical, clean.
- **Usage:** Small labels, coordinates, version numbers, and "technical" callouts.
- **Styling:** All-caps for labels, increased letter-spacing (`0.05em`).

---

## 3. TYPOGRAPHIC SCALE (The Hierarchy)

| Level | Size (rem) | Weight | Line-Height | Usage |
|-------|------------|--------|-------------|-------|
| **H1** | 4.0rem | 800 | 1.1 | Hero Section Main Heading |
| **H2** | 2.5rem | 700 | 1.2 | Section Headings |
| **H3** | 1.5rem | 600 | 1.3 | Subsection Headings |
| **Body L**| 1.125rem | 400 | 1.6 | Primary Body Text |
| **Body S**| 0.875rem | 400 | 1.6 | Secondary / Caption Text |
| **Mono S**| 0.75rem | 500 | 1.4 | Technical Labels / Meta |

---

## 4. APPLICATION RULES

### I. Contrast & Hierarchy
- Never use two different heading sizes in the same visual block.
- Always ensure a clear distinction between "Statement" (H1/H2) and "Detail" (Body).

### II. Alignment
- Headings should be left-aligned for architectural stability or center-aligned for cinematic impact.
- Never use "Justified" text; it creates awkward gaps that break the luxury feel.

---

## 5. QUALITY GATE: TYPO AUDIT
A page is "Typo-Done" only when:
- [ ] The typographic scale is strictly followed.
- [ ] No "random" font sizes are used.
- [ ] The contrast between headings and body is immediate and clear.
- [ ] The Monospace font is used only for technical accents.

*“Typography is the clothes that words wear. Make sure they are tailored.”*
