# 🗺️ PROJECT ROADMAP: THE EVOLUTION OF HEXA VISION

**Version:** 1.0 | **Horizon:** 2024–2026 | **Status:** Strategic Blueprint

## 1. STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE
The roadmap is designed to transition HEXA Vision from a **High-End Portfolio** to a **Comprehensive Architectural Ecosystem**. We are not just building a site; we are building the infrastructure for the future of digital architecture.

---

## 2. THE THREE HORIZONS OF GROWTH

### Horizon 1: The Foundation (The "Luxury Launch")
**Goal:** Establish the "Awwwards" visual bar and a stable enterprise core.
- **Visuals:** Implementation of the 3D Core (R3F) with high-fidelity lighting and textures.
- **Core:** NestJS BFF implementation and Strapi CMS integration.
- **Performance:** Achieving LCP < 1.2s and stable 60 FPS.
- **Outcome:** A world-class digital experience that establishes the brand.

### Horizon 2: The Ecosystem (The "Interactive Portal")
**Goal:** Transition from a static experience to a dynamic, client-centric platform.
- **Client Portal:** Implementation of a secure area for clients to track project progress.
- **Dynamic Content:** Integration of real-time project updates via Strapi webhooks.
- **Advanced Interactivity:** Implementation of a "Designer Mode" for real-time architectural tweaks.
- **Outcome:** A functional tool that adds business value beyond visual storytelling.

### Horizon 3: The Intelligence (The "AI Architect")
**Goal:** Integrate AI to automate and enhance the architectural experience.
- **AI Assistants:** Deployment of CEO, Sales, and PM assistants to handle client journeys.
- **Generative Visualization:** AI-driven lighting and material suggestions.
- **Predictive Analytics:** Using BI to forecast project timelines and resource needs.
- **Outcome:** A market-leading AI-powered architecture platform.

---

## 4. RISK MITIGATION STRATEGY

- **Performance Degradation:** Every new feature is audited against the Performance Budget.
- **Scope Creep:** All new features must be approved via an ADR and mapped to a Horizon.
- **Tech Obsolescence:** Quarterly review of the Tech Stack to evaluate WebGPU or new Next.js versions.
