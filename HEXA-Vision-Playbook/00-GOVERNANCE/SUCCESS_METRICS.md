# 📈 SUCCESS METRICS: THE MEASURE OF EXCELLENCE

**Version:** 1.0 | **Domain:** Governance | **Focus:** KPI & ROI

## 1. THE METRIC PHILOSOPHY
We do not track "vanity metrics" (e.g., total page views). We track **Quality Metrics** that correlate directly with the "Luxury" perception and "Enterprise" stability.

---

## 2. PRIMARY QUALITY METRICS (P-METRICS)

### I. Visual & Performance Score (The "V-Score")
**Formula:** `(Lighthouse Performance + UX Audit Score) / 2`
- **Target:** 95+
- **Why:** High performance is the foundation of luxury. A stuttering 3D scene is a failure.

### II. Conversion & Trust (The "C-Score")
- **Metric:** Time spent on Project Case Studies.
- **Target:** > 3 minutes per project.
- **Why:** High engagement indicates that the storytelling is effective and the user is immersed.

### III. Engineering Health (The "H-Score")
- **Metric:** Bug-to-Feature Ratio.
- **Target:** < 0.1.
- **Why:** Stability is the hallmark of enterprise software.

---

## 3. DETAILED KPI MATRIX

| Dimension | Metric | Target | Frequency |
|-----------|---------|---------|-----------|
| **LCP** | Largest Contentful Paint | < 1.2s | Per Page |
| **FPS** | Frame Rate (3D Scene) | 60 FPS | Continuous |
| **CLS** | Cumulative Layout Shift | < 0.1 | Per Page |
| **TBT** | Total Blocking Time | < 200ms | Per Page |
| **Error Rate** | Sentry Error Frequency | < 0.1% | Daily |
| **Build Time** | CI/CD Pipeline Speed | < 5 mins | Per Push |

---

## 4. THE "LUXURY" AUDIT
Quarterly, the site undergoes a "Luxury Audit" where we compare the experience against current Awwwards winners.
- **Gap Analysis:** Identify where our experience falls short of the latest trends.
- **Iterative Lift:** implement 3 "Elite" upgrades per quarter to maintain the lead.

*“What cannot be measured cannot be improved.”*
