# 🔄 PROJECT LIFECYCLE: FROM VISION TO REALITY

**Version:** 1.0 | **Domain:** Business | **Focus:** Delivery Process

## 1. THE LIFECYCLE PHILOSOPHY
Every project at HEXA Studio follows a rigid, high-quality pipeline. We do not skip steps. We do not "wing it." We follow the **Lifecycle of Excellence**.

---

## 2. THE FIVE STAGES OF DELIVERY

### Stage 1: Initiation (The "Alignment")
**Focus:** Understanding the vision and setting the boundaries.
- **Inputs:** Client brief, moodboards, initial constraints.
- **Outputs:** Project Charter, signed Contract, and the lauch of the `PROJECT_LIFECYCLE.md` log.
- **Quality Gate:** Alignment on the "Luxury" vision.

### Stage 2: Discovery & Design (The "Blueprint")
**Focus:** Defining the a-to-z of the experience.
- **Inputs:** User personas, content strategy, architectural assets.
- **Outputs:** Wireframes, Low-fidelity 3D blocks, Navigation Map.
- **Quality Gate:** Approval of the High-Level Design (HLD).

### Stage 3: Implementation (The "Craft")
**Focus:** Engineering the masterpiece.
- **Inputs:** Approved designs, assets, technical specs.
- **Outputs:** Functional frontend, robust backend, optimized 3D scenes.
- **Quality Gate:** Passing the "Internal" Quality Gate Controller.

### Stage 4: Validation (The "Polish")
**Focus:** Removing every single flaw.
- **Inputs:** Beta version of the site.
- **Outputs:** Bug reports, Performance Audit, UX Audit.
- **Quality Gate:** Scoring 9.5/10 in the UX Audit Framework.

### Stage 5: Deployment & Evolution (The "Legacy")
**Focus:** Launching and maintaining.
- **Inputs:** Final approved build.
- **Outputs:** Live site, handover documentation, maintenance plan.
- **Quality Gate:** Final Client Sign-off.

---

## 3. THE FEEDBACK LOOP
At the end of every stage, a **Post-Mortem** is conducted:
- What went well?
- Where did we struggle?
- How do we improve the process for the next project?

---

## 4. CRITICAL PATH ANALYSIS
Any delay in Stage 2 (Design) creates a compounding delay in Stage 3 (Implementation). Therefore, we prioritize **Design Certainty** over **Coding Speed**.

*“Measure twice, cut once. Design twice, build once.”*
