# 📈 KPIs: KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS

**Version:** 1.0 | **Domain:** Business | **Focus:** Quantitative Success

## 1. THE KPI PHILOSOPHY
We do not track activity; we track **Outcomes**. A KPI is only valuable if it directly correlates with the project's "Luxury" or "Enterprise" goals.

---

## 2. CORE KPIs (THE "VITAL SIGNS")

### I. Quality KPIs (The "Luxury" Metrics)
- **Visual Fidelity Score:** Average score from the UX Audit Framework (Target: 9.5+).
- **Motion Fluidity:** Frame drops per session (Target: < 1%).
- **Brand Alignment:** Consistency score based on the Brand Guardian's audit.

### II. Performance KPIs (The "Speed" Metrics)
- **LCP (Largest Contentful Paint):** Time to first meaningful visual (Target: < 1.2s).
- **CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift):** Visual stability (Target: < 0.1).
- **TTI (Time to Interactive):** How fast the 3D scene becomes responsive (Target: < 2s).

### III. Business KPIs (The "Growth" Metrics)
- **Conversion Rate:** % of visitors who initiate a consultation.
- **Project Cycle Time:** Total time from Initiation to Handover.
- **Client LTV (Lifetime Value):** Total value of projects per client.

---

## 3. MEASUREMENT & REPORTING
KPIs are measured and reported in the `PROJECT_STATUS.md` on a weekly basis.

- **Green (🟢):** Target met or exceeded.
- **Yellow (🟡):** Within 10% of target; requires monitoring.
- **Red (🔴):** Target missed; requires an immediate "Correction Plan."

---

## 4. THE "ZERO-TOLERANCE" METRIC
**The "Crash" Metric:** Any crash in the production environment is a **Critical Failure**. The goal is 99.9% uptime with zero fatal errors.

*“If you can't measure it, you can't manage it.”*
