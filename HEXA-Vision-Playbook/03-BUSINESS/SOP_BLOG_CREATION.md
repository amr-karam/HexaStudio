# SOP-CO-02: Blog Post Creation

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-08  
**Owner:** Content Lead / SEO Specialist  

---

## Goal

To produce and publish high-value architectural insights that drive SEO and establish HEXA Studio as a thought leader in the industry.

## Prerequisites

- Approved topic and outline.
- High-resolution imagery or 3D renders.
- Strapi CMS access.
- SEO keywords list.

## Step-by-Step Process

### 1. Content Drafting
- **Writing:** Draft the post using the "Expert Tone" (Professional, Insightful, Luxury).
- **Structure:** Use H1, H2, and H3 tags for readability.
- **Visuals:** Embed high-res images or links to 3D scenes to illustrate points.
- **Internal Linking:** Link to relevant projects in the gallery.

### 2. SEO Optimization
- **Keywords:** Integrate primary and secondary keywords naturally into the text.
- **Meta Data:** Write a compelling Meta Title (< 60 chars) and Meta Description (< 160 chars).
- **Slug:** Create a clean, keyword-rich URL slug.
- **Alt Text:** Add descriptive alt text to all images for accessibility and SEO.

### 3. Review & Approval
- **Editorial Review:** Check for grammar, tone, and flow.
- **Technical Review:** Verify that any technical claims are accurate.
- **Approval:** Final sign-off from the Creative Director.

### 4. Publishing & Promotion
- **Publish:** Set the post to "Published" in Strapi.
- **Distribution:** Share the post on LinkedIn and Instagram with high-end visuals.
- **Newsletter:** Include the post in the monthly client newsletter.

## Verification

- [ ] Content is free of errors and aligns with the brand voice.
- [ ] SEO meta-tags are correctly implemented.
- [ ] All images have alt text.
- [ ] Links are functional.

## Exception Handling

| Issue | Action |
|-------|---------|
| Image load time high | Re-optimize image or use a CDN |
| Content conflict | Consult the Project Constitution to ensure alignment |
| SEO score low | Re-optimize keywords and meta-data |
