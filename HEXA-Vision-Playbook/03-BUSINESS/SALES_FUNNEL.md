# 🌪️ SALES FUNNEL: THE CONVERSION ENGINE

**Version:** 1.0 | **Domain:** Business | **Focus:** Lead Generation

## 1. THE FUNNEL STRATEGY
We do not use "aggressive" sales. We use **Attraction Marketing**. We attract the world's best architects by showcasing a level of quality that is undeniable.

---

## 2. THE FUNNEL STAGES

### I. TOP OF FUNNEL (ToFu) - Awareness
**Goal:** Maximum visibility among high-end architects.
- **Channels:** Awwwards, Behance, Instagram, LinkedIn.
- **Hook:** "The Future of Architectural Visualization."
- **Metric:** Unique Visitors, Social Shares.

### II. MIDDLE OF FUNNEL (MoFu) - Consideration
**Goal:** Educate the user on the "HEXA Way."
- **Channels:** Case Studies, Blog, "Our Standards" section.
- **Hook:** "How we combine Enterprise Engineering with Luxury Design."
- **Metric:** Average Session Duration, Page Depth.

### III. BOTTOM OF FUNNEL (BoFu) - Decision
**Goal:** Convert a lead into a high-value client.
- **Channels:** Consultation Request, Detailed Proposal.
- **Hook:** "Let's build your masterpiece together."
- **Metric:** Lead Conversion Rate, Average Contract Value.

---

## 3. THE CONVERSION PATHWAY
`Discovery (Social/Awwwards)` $\rightarrow$ `Portfolio (Awe)` $\rightarrow$ `Standards (Trust)` $\rightarrow$ `Consultation (Action)`

---

## 4. LEAD QUALIFICATION (THE "ELITE" FILTER)
Not every client is a fit for HEXA Studio. We qualify leads based on:
1. **Vision Alignment:** Does the client value quality over cost?
2. **Project Scale:** Is the project complex enough to benefit from our approach?
3. **Budget Capacity:** Can the client support a premium, high-end implementation?

*“We don't hunt for clients; we attract the right ones.”*
